# Popout FAQ

## What is Popout?

Popout is the fastest no-code portfolio builder. Pick a template, add projects and experience, publish with a custom domain. Built-in analytics, responsive design, done in 5 minutes.

## Who is it for?

Popout is the no-code portfolio builder for developers, freelancers, and creators who need a good-looking personal site in under five minutes. Pick a template, drop in projects, experience, and socials, publish on your own domain. Built-in analytics show who's viewing your work. A serious alternative to Linktree, About.me, and Webflow for simple portfolios.
